United Village is a project designed to provide an experience of sharing a sense of community and culture from locations around the world.
With each update, we will feature a visual series of moments and aspects of life from various locations.
Will the world continue to converge as transportation and telecommunication advancements simplify global communication?
Or will the world become even more segmented as the income gap between regions widens?
At least we know that with so many disparate civilizations and cultures across the planet, diversity remains an irreplaceable richness.
We hope that the experience of exploring our content is second only to visiting the locations themselves.
