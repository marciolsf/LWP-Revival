Whenever I come to Cuba, I ask myself " what is the "quality of life"? Stunning beach, sugarcane field, expressive faces, advanced medical services/education/engineering, culture diversity, old town, "active" antique cars, and Fidel, absolutely! As the country might not be rich in the face of stats, it is one of the most charming places on the planet.

Masaki Hirano; a globe-trotting photographer from Sarajevo to Tasmania, South Africa, East Timor... reviews Cubans with homage "the most photogenic subject I always respect". Our first "Life Scape" features Habanero and Habanera in early 90's from his outstanding photo book "DOWN THE ROAD OF LIFE".
