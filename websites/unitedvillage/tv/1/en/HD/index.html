Tuvalu is an islands country consisting of 9 atolls in the South Pacific Ocean. It is one of the smallest countries with just 26㎢ area. Most of the land is just a few metre above sea level. As the climate change affects seriously, the country is facing a crisis of submersion.

Mr. Shuuichi Endou, a representative of a NGO Tuvalu Overview is leading an ambitious project "10000 Tuvaluan" aiming to photograph all the inhabitants. How the islands will look in the eyes of future generation? "Although the rising sea levels often attack my home island, I love life on this island.
It is my dream to spend my life and die here." (Noataga, a fisherman)
